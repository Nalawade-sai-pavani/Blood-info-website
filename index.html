Normal Blood Levels

  • Hemoglobin (Men): 13.5–17.5 g/dL
  • Hemoglobin (Women): 12–15.5 g/dL
  • WBC: 4,000–11,000 /µL
  • Platelets: 150,000–450,000 /µL
  • RBC: 4.2–6.1 million/µL
